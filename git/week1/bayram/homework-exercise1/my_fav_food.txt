How to Make a Classic Omelette

Ingredients:
- 3 large eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional fillings (cheese, mushrooms, ham, etc.)

Instructions:
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until well combined
2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat
3. Add butter and let it melt
4. Pour in the beaten eggs
5. As eggs begin to set, use a spatula to gently push cooked edges toward center
6. When eggs are almost set, add your desired fillings to one half
7. Fold the empty half over the filled side
8. Cook for another minute until the cheese melts (if using)
9. Slide onto a plate and serve immediately

Enjoy your delicious omelette!
