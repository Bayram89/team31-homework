Risalamande

Preparation time: 30 mins | Total time: 4 hours | Yield: 5-6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 lt whole milk
  • 180 gr rice porridge
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 70 gr sugar
  • 100 gr almonds
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 400 ml whipping cream

Instructions

    1. Put milk and rice in a pot. Slowly heat it up, stirring often.

    2. Turn the heat to low, stir for a few minutes, then cover with a lid.

    3. Scrape seeds from a vanilla pod and mix them with sugar. Add the seeds, sugar, and the empty pod to the pot.

    4. Stir and let it simmer for 45-50 minute until the rice is soft. Remove the vanilla pod.

    5. Pour boiling water over almonds, let them sit for 10 minutes, then drain.

    6. Repeat with fresh boiling water for 2 minutes. Drain, peel, and chop them.

    7. Mix the chopped almonds and a pinch of salt into the pudding.

    8. Let it cool in the fridge for a few hours or overnight.

    9. Whip cream until fluffy and fold it into the pudding. Serve cold.

    This makes a creamy, sweet rice pudding with a nice crunch from the almonds!
